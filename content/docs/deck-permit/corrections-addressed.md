## Narrative Summary

This resubmission addresses all seven plan-examiner items by replacing the original one-sheet schematic with a full permit package that specifies sizes, connections, spans, cantilevers, and footing details with direct references to RCO 507 and the Franklin County Deck Guide tables. The structural design has also been re-confirmed against a 2×8-ledger constraint (the house's existing 2×8 band joist caps joist depth): 2×8 SP #2 PT joists at 16" o.c. span 11'-7" from ledger to a single 2-ply 2×8 SP beam, with a 2'-3" cantilever to the outer edge — all within RCO Table 507.6 limits. Additional work not originally drawn but now included: clipped-corner framing at the two outer corners (angled rim joists with Simpson LSSU210 skewed hangers), stair and paver landing on the east side (RCO 311.7 compliant), full-perimeter guardrail (RCO 312), the ⅛"-per-foot deck slope, and decommissioning of an unused gas stub that currently notches the old ledger (coordinated under a separate Franklin County Public Health mechanical/gas permit with a licensed Ohio plumbing contractor).
