## Lot & Setbacks

Deck footprint is unchanged from the existing approved footprint — no zoning variance is required. The deck edge is the binding structural boundary. Stairs project ~33" east of the east deck rim and the paver landing adds another 36"; this projects into the 42'-wide east side yard with ~36' of clearance remaining, so stair/landing projection does not drive the setback analysis. At-grade pavers are treated as landscape flatwork, not a structure, per standard local practice.
