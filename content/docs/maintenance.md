# Maintenance

Recurring upkeep organized by frequency, season, and month.

## Weekly (Growing Season)
- Mow lawn (Mar–Nov)

## Monthly
- Test smoke and CO detectors (press test button)
- Freshen garbage disposal (ice cubes + citrus rinds)
- Wipe washer gasket and detergent drawer

## Spring

### March
- Mower readiness: oil change, sharpen blade, replace spark plug, fresh fuel, clean/replace air filter, clean deck, check tire pressure
- Pressure washer readiness: pump oil change, inspect hoses and O-rings, check connections, flush pump
- First lawn fertilizer application
- Furnace filter change
- HVAC tune-up (AC pre-season service)
- Refrigerator coil and grille clean
- Sump pump test
- Landscape cleanout

### April
- Carve edges
- Tree and shrub trim
- Pressure wash house and sidewalk
- New mulch
- Dryer vent cleaning
- Gutter cleaning (post-pollen)
- Wash exterior and interior windows + screens
- Ceiling fans: dust and set summer direction (counterclockwise)
- Washer and dishwasher deep clean cycle

### May
- Sidewalk edging
- Deck inspect, clean, stain or seal as needed
- Outdoor furniture clean and set out
- Refrigerator water filter replacement

## Summer

### June
- Lawn fertilizer
- Furnace filter change
- Caulk inspection (kitchen, bath)
- Grout sealing where worn

### July
- Showerhead descale
- Bathroom exhaust fan clean
- Test GFCI outlets
- Washer and dishwasher deep clean cycle

### August
- Wash garbage and recycle bins
- Range hood filter degrease
- Vacuum/clean refrigerator water pan area

## Fall

### September
- Lawn fertilizer
- Furnace filter change
- Sidewalk edging touch-up
- HVAC tune-up (furnace pre-season service)
- Chimney inspection / sweep
- Wash exterior and interior windows

### October
- Leaf cleanup
- Roof and flashing inspection
- Fall gutter cleaning (post leaf-drop)
- Caulk and weather stripping inspection (windows, doors)
- Ceiling fans: set winter direction (clockwise)
- Refrigerator water filter replacement
- Washer and dishwasher deep clean cycle

### November
- Mower winter prep: drain fuel or add stabilizer, disconnect/remove battery, clean deck, sharpen blade for next year
- Pressure washer winter prep: drain pump, add pump saver/antifreeze, drain fuel or add stabilizer, disconnect and drain hoses
- Final lawn fertilizer (winterizer)
- Drain and store outdoor hoses
- Shut off and drain outdoor faucets
- Replace smoke detector batteries

## Winter

### December
- Furnace filter change
- Water heater flush
- Inspect attic for ice-dam signs after first storms

### January
- Washer and dishwasher deep clean cycle
- Mattress flip/rotate
- Garage door lubrication and safety sensor test
- Replace garage door keypad batteries
- Check fire extinguishers (date and gauge)

### February
- Oven deep clean (self-clean or manual)
- Deep clean behind/around dryer
- Vacuum HVAC return grilles and registers
- Plan spring projects, order any tooling or materials
